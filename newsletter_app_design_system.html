Why we define color scales from 100–900
  • Consistent light-to-dark progression
  • Easier mapping for design + dev
  • Supports accessibility + theming (including dark mode)
  • Avoids inconsistent labels like “light”, “lighter”, “darkest”

Matches the newsletter sample: headings use Raleway/Roboto, body uses Lato. H1/H2 are 24px; paragraphs are 16px.
